About Us

CareerAffix is an organisation dedicated to assisting individuals with their career-related issues. Our mission is to provide a comprehensive platform for sharing knowledge, resources, and opportunities, enabling candidates to achieve their career aspirations.Our vision is to empower job seekers, students, and career changers to succeed in the competitive and ever-changing job market by providing a quality career assessment programme.

Our team of experts combines industry knowledge, career passion, and personalised attention to help you achieve your career goals and dreams.
